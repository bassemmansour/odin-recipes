"Hummus, the ultimate dip!"

That delicious hummus dish!

is a Middle Eastern dip, spread, or savory dish made from cooked, mashed chickpeas blended with tahini, lemon juice, and garlic. The standard garnish in the Middle East includes olive oil, a few whole chickpeas, parsley, and paprika.

Ingredients

  • 200 grams of dried chickpeas
  • 100 grams of Tahini
  • 1 limon juice
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 tea spoon of salt
  • 1/2 tea spoon of paprica

Steps

  1. wash the chickpeas with fresh water, then rinse for 12 hours.
  2. Boil the checkpeas till achievinga soft mashable consistency.
  3. Drain and keep asisde to cool down to room temperature. (Keep the drained water for futher use to control consistency of hummus.)
  4. Using a food processor, blend the checkpeas with all other ingredients until achieving a spreadable paste. (Add checkpeas drained water as needed to control consistency as needed.)
